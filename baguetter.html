Owner of Thaumiel

Mr. Baguetter, also known as Ender, owns and runs both the Thaumiel Discord community and SCP: SL server. He is an active and communicative member in our community. He reads all suggestions and requests.
He originally started playing on The Shy Guy One in June of 2023, which was formerly a popular SCP: SL server that shut down in December.
His creation, Thaumiel, is intended to be an attempt at recreating the same feeling with staff improvements, superior communication, and better balance.

He knows a bit about coding. Coding most of the website. He mainly knows HTML and a small bit of C#.
